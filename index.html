Licensing & Technical Requirements

Q: How many devices can I use this on?

Your license key works on up to 2 devices. You can deactivate a device anytime in Settings to free up a slot and activate on a different computer.

Q: What are the system requirements?

Alpha Forge Studio runs on Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit). You'll need:

  • 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended for large bulk exports)
  • 500MB free disk space
  • Internet connection for activation and periodic verification

Q: Does this work on Mac?

No. Alpha Forge Studio is currently Windows only (Windows 10 or later). Mac support may be added in a future update, but there is no timeline. If you need Mac support, this software is not for you at this time.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?

An internet connection is required for initial activation. After that, the app checks in periodically to verify your license. You can work offline between check-ins, but this is not a fully offline app — it does require periodic internet access.

Core Features & Workflow

Q: What can I create with Alpha Forge Studio?

Alpha Forge Studio is designed for creating:

  • Alphabet sets (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
  • Custom shapes (butterflies, hearts, paws, any template you design)
  • Digital paper collections
  • Clipart bundles with consistent designs across all characters

Q: How does the workflow work?

Single Design:

  1. Drag and drop a design image onto the canvas
  2. Preview appears instantly
  3. Customize colors and outlines as needed
  4. Export individual letters or the full set

Bulk Design:

  1. Load multiple design images at once
  2. Click Bulk Export
  3. Each design generates a complete alphabet set with mockups automatically
  4. Everything exports to organized folders

Q: What customization options do I have?

  • Fill colors — apply solid colors to letters
  • Outline colors — customize primary and secondary outline colors
  • Design images — use any PNG, JPG, or WEBP image as your design
  • Transparent designs — add colored fills behind transparent images
  • Surface textures — add texture overlays with adjustable strength

All changes preview in real-time on the canvas.

Q: Can I save unlimited colors in my palette?

Yes! Save unlimited brand colors to your custom palette and use them for fills, outlines, and bulk color exports. Your saved colors persist between sessions.

Export & Organization Features

Q: What is Bulk Export by Color Palette?

One of the most powerful features for creating product lines. Save your brand colors to the palette, load an alpha set or shape, apply a fill color, then export across every color variation with a single click.

Example: Load your alpha set, save 20 brand colors → get 20 complete sets instantly, each in a different fill color and organized into its own folder.

Q: How are my exports organized?

Alpha Forge Studio automatically organizes your exports into folders so everything is ready for uploading to your shop. You can also add custom prefixes and suffixes to filenames and toggle subfolder organization on or off.

Q: Can I trim transparent edges from my exports?

Yes! There's a built-in option to trim transparent edges automatically during export for cleaner, more professional files.

Q: What file formats does it export?

Alpha Forge Studio exports PNG with transparency and optional layered PSD files. Your original DPI is always preserved, and files are ready to upload to Etsy, Creative Fabrica, Creative Market, or any marketplace.

Mockups & Automation

Q: How do the auto-generated mockups work?

When you bulk export, the app automatically generates professional listing mockups for every design — no Canva, no Photoshop, no extra tools needed.

Key mockup features:

  • Multiple mockup layouts included
  • Export with transparent backgrounds for use on any backdrop
  • Mockups respect your fill colors and design choices
  • Ready to upload directly to your shop listings

Q: Can I use multiple designs at once?

Yes! Load multiple design images and the app creates a complete alphabet set with mockups for each one in a single batch.

Example: 10 designs = 10 complete alphabet sets with mockups, all in one export.

Alpha Sets & Getting Started

Q: What alpha set formats does it support?

The app works with layered alpha sets that include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Starter alpha sets are included so you can begin creating immediately.

Q: How do I prepare my own alpha sets?

Use the built-in Prepare Set tool — just drop your raw files in and the app organizes them for you. Your download also includes a README.txt with setup instructions if you need additional guidance.

Q: Do I need Photoshop?

No! Alpha Forge Studio handles everything — file preparation, design application, and export — all within the app. No external software required.

Q: Can I organize my alpha sets into collections?

Yes! Group your sets by style or project and switch between them instantly using the dropdown menu.
